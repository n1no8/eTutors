Services We Provide

For Pupils Preparing for 11+ Exams

For our younger pupils we focus on four core subjects, those being English, Mathematics, Verbal reasoning and Non-Verbal reasoning. We aim to build on core skills that will that will not only benefit them in their preparation for their 11+ entrance exams but also foster confidence and ensure that they begin secondary school with a running start. Our tailored approach to tutoring helps identify and strengthen individual weaknesses while encouraging a deeper understanding of the core subjects.

For Pupils Preparing for GCSEs

For secondary school pupils we offer tuition in a wider range of subjects, those being Mathematics, English, Physics, Chemistry and Biology. With our older students our approach is more tailored to their academic goals rather than focusing solely on developing foundational skills, we aim to deepen their understanding of complex topics and help them master exam techniques.

How It Works

A brief breakdown on how our tuition services work!

TIMETABLING

The tutor assigned to your child will work with you to arrange lesson times that fit seamlessly into their schedule. Depending on your child's needs, this could range from a single lesson per week to multiple sessions if additional support is required. We pride ourselves on being flexible, and if unforeseen circumstances arise, we are more than happy to reschedule lessons to ensure your child doesn’t miss out on valuable learning time.

CONNECTING THROUGH ZOOM

At the start of each scheduled lesson, your assigned tutor will send you a Zoom link to join the session, which would typically last for one hour. Based on our experience, we’ve found that younger pupils tend to have shorter attention spans, making shorter lessons much more productive.
